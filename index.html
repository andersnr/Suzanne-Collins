Suzanne Collins

International bestselling author Suzanne Collins began her professional career writing for children's television. She worked on the staffs of several Nickelodeon shows, including the Emmy-nominated hit Clarissa Explains it All and The Mystery Files of Shelby Woo. She is now an internatioinal bestseller with the Hunger Games series, with a recent fourth book added to the trilogy.
